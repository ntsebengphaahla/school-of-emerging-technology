Welcome to the School of emerging technologies

The School of Emerging Technologies is committed to shaping the future of each student’s creativity, technology skills, and problem solving skills. The focus of our programs is to provide the maximum empowerment through modern curriculum, active participation, and collaboration across specialties. Our new age offerings range from artificial intelligence to computing with sustainability. Our students take pride in emerging as experts from our STEM focused education model which leverages real-world challenges alongside technologies guided by industry veteran faculty.
